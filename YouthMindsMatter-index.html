About Video Articles Resources

Youth Minds matter is a project created by youth for youth - a space designed for both students and parents to explore the topic of mental health. Here, you’ll find valuable tips, professional resources and discover community support to help you or your kid in navigating the world of learning with a positive mindset.

What is Mental Health?

Mental health, in simple terms, is about your long-term feelings and thoughts, how you handle stress, and your ability to connect with others. It’s about the capacity to navigate life’s ups and downs while still having a positive self-image and outlook on life.
